Data & Storage

Every single day, humans generate approximately 2.5 quintillion bytes of data. To put that in perspective, 90% of all the data in the world was created in just the last two years.

Fun Fact About Emails

Over 300 billion emails are sent every single day around the world. That is roughly 3.5 million emails sent every second!
