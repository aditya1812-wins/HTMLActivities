Science Fact Sheet

This page demonstrates every HTML text formatting tag using real science facts.

1. Bold — <b>

The speed of light is exactly 299,792,458 metres per second.

2. Strong — <strong>

Warning: UV radiation can cause permanent eye and skin damage.

3. Italic — <i>

The scientific name of humans is Homo sapiens.

4. Emphasis — <em>

The brain is not fully developed until age 25.

5. Highlight — <mark>

Water can exist in three states at once at the triple point of water.

6. Underline — <u>

Hydrogen makes up 75% of all normal matter in the universe.

7. Strikethrough — <s>

Our solar system has nine planets eight planets.

8. Deleted — <del>

Age of Earth: 6,000 years → 4.54 billion years.

9. Inserted — <ins>

Atoms are made of protons, neutrons, and electrons.

10. Small — <small>

Your DNA would stretch 60x to the Sun and back. * Approximate estimate based on 37 trillion cells.

11. Subscript — <sub>

Water = H2O   Carbon Dioxide = CO2   Glucose = C6H12O6

12. Superscript — <sup>

Einstein's equation: E = mc2   Observable stars: 2 × 1023

13. Blockquote — <blockquote>

"The important thing is not to stop questioning."

— Albert Einstein

14. Abbreviation — <abbr>

DNA carries genetic instructions for all living organisms.

15. Code — <code>

Binary for 10 is 1010. One byte = 8 bits.
